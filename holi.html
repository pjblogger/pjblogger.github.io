Holi 2077 B.S.

Holi is one of the most popular festival celebrated by Hindus.It is also known as “festival of spring” as well as “festival of love”. It is celebrated from Sunday,March 28 and ends on Monday,March 29.The celebration of this festival contains lots of ancient beliefs such as divine love of Radha and Krishna. It also signifies the triumph of good over evil.


5 facts about holi:

1. It is a festival of equality.


2. It is celebrated in South Asian countries especially in Nepal and India.


3. Death of Holika was also the cause of holi celebration.


4. People pray around a bonfire on this day.


5. People also drink Bhang juice on this day.

How to celebrate holi safely during covid-19 pandemic?


  1. Maintain distance of at least 3 metres while throwing water balloons.
  2. Better to enjoy delicious foods at home rather than going out.
  3. Bath properly after holi celebration.
  4. Stay away from people suffering from cough and cold.
  5. Use gloves and don’t catch cold.
